## 📊 Dashboard

The Dashboard is your command center for fuel management.

### Setting Up Your Trip

1. **Fuel Level Slider**
   - Drag to set current fuel percentage
   - Updates predictions in real-time

2. **Distance to Destination**
   - Enter how far you need to travel (in km)
   - Used for risk calculation

3. **Traffic Condition**
   - Light: Free-flowing traffic
   - Normal: Regular traffic
   - Moderate: Some congestion
   - Heavy: Significant delays
   - Traffic Jam: Standstill traffic

4. **Terrain Type**
   - Downhill: Improves fuel efficiency
   - Flat: Standard consumption
   - Slight Uphill: Minor increase
   - Uphill: Significant increase
   - Steep: Maximum consumption

### Understanding Predictions

**Risk Meter:**
- 🟢 **SAFE**: You can reach comfortably
- 🟡 **CAUTION**: Close call, consider refueling
- 🟠 **WARNING**: Risky, refuel recommended
- 🔴 **DANGER**: Cannot reach, refuel immediately

**Key Metrics:**
- **Safe Range**: Distance you can safely travel (with 30% buffer)
- **Adjusted Range**: Realistic range considering conditions
- **Base Range**: Theoretical maximum range
- **Risk Score**: 0-100 safety score
- **Confidence Score**: Prediction accuracy (0-100%)

**Recommendations:**
- Critical alerts for dangerous situations
- Suggestions for optimal refueling
- Safety reminders

### Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can:
- 🗺️ **View Map**: See nearby stations
- 🚨 **Emergency**: Request immediate help
- 👨‍🔧 **Find Help**: Browse professionals
- 💬 **AI Assistant**: Ask questions

## 🚗 Vehicle Setup

Accurate vehicle information = Better predictions!

### Adding Your Vehicle

1. **Go to Vehicle Setup** from the menu
2. **Select Vehicle Type:**
   - 🏍️ Bike (45 km/l average)
   - 🚗 Car (15 km/l average)
   - 🚙 SUV (12 km/l average)
   - 🚚 Truck (5 km/l average)
   - 🚌 Bus (4 km/l average)
   - ⚡ EV (120 km/kWh average)

3. **Choose Fuel Type:**
   - Petrol
   - Diesel
   - Electric
   - CNG
   - Hybrid

4. **Enter Mileage:**
   - Your actual vehicle mileage
   - Check your vehicle manual
   - Or use average from recent trips

5. **Tank Capacity:**
   - Total fuel tank size
   - For EVs: battery capacity in kWh

6. **Driving Style:**
   - 🌱 Eco: Fuel-efficient driving (+10%)
   - 🚗 Normal: Standard driving
   - ⚡ Aggressive: Fast acceleration (-25%)
   - 🏎️ Sport: Performance mode (-35%)

7. **Optional Details:**
   - Brand (e.g., Toyota, Honda)
   - Model (e.g., Camry, Civic)
   - Year

8. **Click "Save Vehicle"**

### Editing Your Vehicle

- Go to Vehicle Setup anytime
- Update any details
- Changes apply immediately to predictions

## 🚨 Emergency Features

Help when you need it most.

### SOS Alert

**When to use:**
- Immediate danger
- Medical emergency
- Vehicle breakdown in unsafe location

**How to use:**
1. Go to Emergency section
2. Click **"SEND SOS ALERT"**
3. Alert sent to:
   - Your emergency contacts
   - Local emergency services
   - Your location shared

### Emergency Service Requests

**Available Services:**

1. **⛽ Fuel Delivery**
   - Emergency fuel brought to you
   - Typical arrival: 15-30 minutes
   - Fee: ₹100-500

2. **🔧 Mechanic**
   - On-site vehicle repair
   - Typical arrival: 10-20 minutes
   - Fee: ₹500-2000

3. **🚛 Tow Service**
   - Vehicle transport
   - Typical arrival: 15-30 minutes
   - Fee: ₹1000-5000

4. **🏥 Medical Help**
   - First aid or ambulance
   - Immediate response
   - Fee: Varies

**Request Process:**
1. Click the service you need
2. System searches for nearby help
3. Helper assigned (usually within 3-5 seconds)
4. Track arrival time
5. Helper contacts you

**Canceling a Request:**
- Click "Cancel Request" if situation changes
- No penalty for cancellation

### Emergency Contacts

**Managing Contacts:**
1. View default emergency numbers (112, 100, 108)
2. Add personal contacts:
   - Click "+ Add Emergency Contact"
   - Enter name and phone number
   - Save
3. Quick call any contact

**Best Practices:**
- Add at least 3 emergency contacts
- Include family members
- Add local contacts for frequent routes
- Keep contacts updated

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### Fuel Management

1. **Never let fuel drop below 25%**
   - Protects fuel pump
   - Provides emergency buffer
   - Reduces stress

2. **Plan refueling stops**
   - Before entering remote areas
   - When safe range < 1.5× distance
   - At convenient locations

3. **Monitor conditions**
   - Check traffic before departure
   - Consider weather impact
   - Adjust for terrain

### Driving Efficiency

1. **Eco Driving Style**
   - Smooth acceleration
   - Maintain steady speed
   - Anticipate stops
   - Can improve efficiency by 10-15%

2. **Reduce Load**
   - Remove unnecessary items
   - Empty roof racks when not in use
   - Lighter = better mileage

3. **Maintenance**
   - Regular servicing
   - Proper tire pressure
   - Clean air filters
   - Well-maintained = efficient

### Safety

1. **Before Long Trips**
   - Full fuel tank
   - Check vehicle condition
   - Share location with family
   - Save emergency contacts

2. **During Journey**
   - Monitor fuel regularly
   - Take breaks every 2 hours
   - Stay hydrated
   - Keep phone charged

3. **Emergency Preparedness**
   - First aid kit in vehicle
   - Basic tools
   - Flashlight
   - Emergency numbers saved

### App Usage

1. **Keep Vehicle Info Updated**
   - Accurate mileage
   - Current driving style
   - Any vehicle changes

2. **Use All Features**
   - Check map before trips
   - Set up emergency contacts
   - Try AI assistant
   - Explore marketplace

3. **Provide Feedback**
   - Rate professionals
   - Report issues
   - Suggest improvements
