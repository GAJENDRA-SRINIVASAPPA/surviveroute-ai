## 🚀 Core Features (Standard)

### 1. Intelligent Fuel Prediction
- **Accuracy**: 85-95%
- **Factors**: Traffic, terrain, weather, driving style, load, AC usage
- **Learning**: Improves with every trip
- **Real-time**: Updates continuously

### 2. Interactive Map System
- **Displays**: Fuel stations, EV chargers, prices, ratings
- **Filters**: By type, distance, availability
- **Offline**: Works without internet (cached data)
- **Visual**: Shows safe fuel range zones

### 3. Emergency Assistance
- **SOS**: One-tap emergency alert
- **Services**: Fuel delivery, mechanic, tow, medical
- **Tracking**: Real-time helper location
- **Contacts**: Auto-notify emergency contacts

### 4. Professional Marketplace
- **Find**: Verified mechanics, tow services, fuel delivery
- **Rate**: Blockchain-verified ratings (tamper-proof)
- **Request**: Instant service booking
- **Track**: Service provider arrival in real-time

### 5. AI Assistant
- **Chat**: Natural language interface
- **Smart**: Context-aware responses
- **Helpful**: Instant answers to questions
- **Learning**: Remembers your preferences

### 6. Multi-Vehicle Support
- **Types**: Bike, car, SUV, truck, bus, EV
- **Custom**: Enter your exact mileage
- **Profiles**: Manage multiple vehicles
- **Sync**: Cloud backup of all data
